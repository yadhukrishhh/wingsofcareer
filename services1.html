What is JEE?

JEE stands for Joint Entrance Examination, a highly competitive entrance exam conducted in India for admission to undergraduate engineering programs in prestigious institutions like the IITs, NITs, IIITs, and other centrally funded technical institutions. It is divided into two parts :
1. JEE Main: The first stage, which serves as a qualifying exam for JEE Advanced and as a direct entrance test for various engineering colleges.
2. JEE Advanced: The second stage, which is exclusively for candidates aspiring to join the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs)..
